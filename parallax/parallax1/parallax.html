Charlotte, North Carolina

History

Charlotte, North Carolina was founded in 1768 by Scottish-Irish settelers. It was named after the King George III's wife Queen Charlotte which is where the city's nickname "the queen city" comes from. Charlotte is also located in the 38th largest county in North Carolina, but is the largest city in North Carolina in population and land area. In fact it is so larget that eclipses NC's capital and second largest city by almost twice in both population and area.
